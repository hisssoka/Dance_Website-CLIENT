DAILY YOGA, DANCE & FITNESS CLASSES FOR ADULTS & CHILDREN

Welcome to Monsoon Dance where we are committed to high quality instruction of Yoga, Bollywood and Indian Dance forms in a fun & friendly environment for adults and children.

Our programs suit all levels of skill, fitness, interest and dance backgrounds.

We are proud to call Monsoon Dance studio our home in North Austin, which is the first ever studio in Austin with focus on Yoga & Bollywood dance together. We also teach in South Austin and Circle C areas at our satellite locations.

We offer a variety of programs and services to provide our clients and students many flexible and hassle-free options to learn and enjoy our classes. From single class passes to weekly sessions to unlimited class monthly options along with economical packages for family enrollment, you can find the program just right for you!

IF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN RENTING OUR SPACE ($25-$30 PER HOUR), WE WOULD LOVE TO HAVE YOU!

IF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN SCHEDULING DANCE OR YOGA LESSONS WITH OUR INSTRUCTORS JUST FOR YOURSELF OR YOUR GROUP, YOU CAN SCHEDULE PRIVATE LESSONS (ONLY $50 PER SESSION)
